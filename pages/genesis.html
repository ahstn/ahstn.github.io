Genesis Distributions

Business Accounting & Management Software

Overview

A accounting and business management tool designed with the intention of making the employees working days at Genesis Distribution easier and more efficient. Personally I handled the Customer and Repairs side of the business.

The features that I implemented were adding, deleting, displaying and updating customers, their orders and repairs, with various searching and filtering methods when displaying the data. I also included two reporting methods to give the owner easy access to collections of data he could print.

The application was created using C# with WPF being used for the user interface while interacting with a SQL database which contained various stored procedures. Also I used a private git repository for version control.
